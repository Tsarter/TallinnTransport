Segment Speeds

Speeds are found between two sequential GPS points (called segment), speeds are shown for all segments in a given timespan.

Grid Speeds

Speeds are aggregated into fixed-sized cells, and the average speed of each cell is shown on a map.

Speed Graph

Speeds for one vehicle is shown for whole day on a graph.
Its recommended to first open segment speeds and them click on a segment and then this view opens up

Between A and B

Finds all GPS routes between two points, shows statistics.
Export to Excel or Google Sheet to anaylise more.
